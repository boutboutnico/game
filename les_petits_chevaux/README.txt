=== Rules ===
- One horse per cell
- Roll dice again when it is equal to 6
- Kill adversary horse when move finish on cell alreday taken by adversary horse

- Parameters
	-	Player can choose its home position
	-	Bounce against other player or overtake them
	-	Bounce against home position after a complete turn or stop right before stairs entry
